About FIR Draft System

This system helps users prepare and manage FIR drafts in a structured, professional format.

The platform allows users to enter complaint details, add accused information, generate an FIR draft, view printed records, and maintain a history of generated FIRs.

It is designed to simplify documentation while keeping the output clear and formal.
